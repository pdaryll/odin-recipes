Beef Stroganoff

This rich and creamy beef stroganoff recipe is made with ground beef and served with egg noodles. It's super simple to prepare, and ready to serve in under 30 minutes making it a great choice for busy weeknights. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound (450g) beef sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 8 ounces (225g) white mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup (240ml) beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) sour cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)
  • 8 ounces (225g) egg noodles or rice (for serving)

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the sliced beef and cook until browned. Remove the beef from the skillet and set it aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add chopped onion and cook until it becomes translucent.
  4. Add minced garlic and sliced mushrooms, then saut√© until the mushrooms are tender and any liquid has evaporated.
  5. Sprinkle the flour over the mushroom mixture and stir to combine. Cook for a minute to eliminate the raw flour taste.
  6. Pour in the beef broth, Worcestershire sauce, and Dijon mustard. Stir and let the mixture simmer until it thickens.
  7. Return the cooked beef to the skillet and simmer until it's heated through and the sauce is thickened to your liking.
  8. Stir in the sour cream and cook for a few more minutes, but do not let it boil.
  9. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  10. Serve the beef stroganoff over cooked egg noodles or rice.
  11. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley before serving.
